  • Overview

    Embrace change, effortlessly with Heat Free Hair’s premium Tape-Ins for 3C-4A natural hair! 

    Our new, 100% virgin hair Tape-Ins are semi-permanent extensions, providing endless versatility. With a seamless, undetectable blend, take your style possibilities to the next level. From a polished ponytail to flawless wand curls, our reusable Tape-Ins will take you from day to night in minutes!  

    Pre-taped and ready for installation, tape-ins are one of the quickest ways to wear extensions, without the need to conceal your natural hair. Blend the tape-ins between your natural hair for the most lightweight and tension-free install. With proper application, you can explore styles unique to you for 4-8 weeks with ease and minimal effort!

    Enjoy 20 tapes per set (50g), made with premium, flexible, and durable adhesive. Each tape is perfectly sized at 1” to help you discreetly build your desired level of fullness, from natural to glam.

    For a natural full look that mimics a traditional sew-in, we recommend 4-5 sets of tape-ins. For moderate enhancements to your natural hair, we recommend 2-3 sets of tape-ins.

    *Installation and removal by a licensed professional stylist is highly recommended. Tape-Ins are offered in their natural, virgin color only which ranges from a medium to dark brown. 

    New to extensions? Click here to learn more about tape-ins and recommended care tips! 

  • Shipping & returns

    Orders will ship the same business day if placed Monday- Friday before 11 AM EST, excluding holidays and sale periods. We work with trusted shipping carriers, but recommend purchasing Route Package Protection to insure your package, as Heat Free Hair not responsible for lost, stolen, or damaged packages. All orders with hair extensions require a signature for delivery.


    Heat Free Hair is pleased to offer returns for store credit within 14 days of delivery, as long as your order remains in its original condition. To be eligible for a return, products may not be untied, washed, installed, chemically processed, excessively brushed/finger-combed, or tampered with in any way. We do not offer cash refunds. All wigs are final sale.

    If you need assistance with a purchase, send an email to customercare@heatfreehair.com or give us a call at 1-877-686-9044. We are happy to assist you!
